About Us

APU Peace Builders was founded on the belief that meaningful change starts at the local level. We bring together students from diverse backgrounds to collaborate on projects that benefit the local community while fostering deep intercultural bonds.

Our activities range from organizing local beach clean-ups and volunteering at community centers, to hosting dialogue sessions on global peace initiatives. Whether you are an international student looking to connect with the local community, or a domestic student eager to engage globally, there is a place for you here.
